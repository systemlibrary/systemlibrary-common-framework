# Extensions

A lof of common extensions for any application, heres a sample list of the most useful ones:

## Samples
StringExtensions:
- Is() and IsNot()
- ToDateTime()
- ToDate()
- ToMd5()
- ToBase64() and FromBase64()
- Compress() and Decompress()
- Obfuscate() and Unobfuscate()
- ToPascalCase() and toCamelCase()
- MaxLength()
- GetCompressedKey()

ConcurrentDictionaryExtensions:
- Cache()

StringBuilderExtensions:
- Is() and IsNot()
- IndexOf()
- EndsWith()
- GetCompressedKey()

IListExtensions:
- Is() and IsNot()
